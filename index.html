Your features

The best specialists work in our kindergarten, who monitor the physical and psychological balance of the kids, help to master and improve their skills and knowledge.
We have created such adaptation conditions that parents can always be near the baby until he gets used to the new space. Also, we set up a well-coordinated work of educators, mentors, parents and a psychologist for the best interest of the baby in visiting the garden.
Our center has CCTV cameras installed in all classrooms and groups, sensory room and dining room. Parents have the opportunity to monitor their child online through a special application and be confident in the emotional and physical safety of the baby.
Children in our kindergarten eat 4 times a day. Every week we make a new menu, taking into account some of the wishes of the parents.
We care about the safety of our children, so our center has a nurse who monitors the health of children and the general condition of the premises where they are during the day.
